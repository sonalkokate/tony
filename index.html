Bio

He keeps track of his appointments by using a paper agenda. His
patients book appointments with him by calling his personal
cellphone. When he's with a patient he can't answer the phone
and many potential appointments end up being missed calls.

He wants to find an easy and more efficient way to manage with
appointments.
